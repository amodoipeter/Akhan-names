AKAN NAMES

This application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born & depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. In Ghana, children are given their first name(Akan name) according to the day in the week they were born.

Here are some Akan Names, Take a step to get yours too!:

Male:

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female:

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
